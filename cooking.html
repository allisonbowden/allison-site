high-protein shiitake, broccoli & feta egg bites

adapted from NYT Cooking's Cottage Cheese Egg Bites by Naz Deravian

a high-protein, Starbucks-inspired breakfast bite that takes less than 5 minutes of prep time. blending 2% cottage cheese, liquid egg whites, and Trader Joe's fat-free feta yields a velvety, custardy texture with maximum protein and minimal fat.

Prep5 min
Cook25 min
Yields12 bites
Serving2 bites

ingredients

the blender base

  • 4 large whole eggs
  • ½ cup (4 fl oz) liquid egg whites
  • 1¼ cups (10 oz / ~280g) 2% low-fat cottage cheese
  • 1 cup (~110g) Trader Joe's Lite Mexican-Style Shredded Cheese Blend

the toppings

  • ½ cup Trader Joe's frozen sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • ½ cup Trader Joe's frozen broccoli florets, chopped
  • ¼ cup Trader Joe's fat-free feta cheese, crumbled
  • Cooking spray (olive oil or avocado oil)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

instructions

  1. Prep the oven & pan. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Generously spray a 12-cup silicone or nonstick muffin tin with oil spray to prevent sticking.
  2. Blend the base. Add the whole eggs, liquid egg whites, cottage cheese, and lite Mexican-style cheese to a high-speed blender. Blend on high for 8–10 seconds, just until completely smooth. Tip from NYT Cooking: avoid overblending. too much air makes the bites puff up in the oven and collapse as they cool.
  3. Pour the base. Pour the blended egg mixture evenly into the 12 muffin cups, filling each about ¾ of the way full.
  4. Top with veggies & feta. Divide the shiitake mushrooms, broccoli florets, and crumbled feta evenly over each cup. Finish with a pinch of black pepper.
  5. Bake for 22–25 minutes, until the centers are fully set and the edges are light golden brown.
  6. Cool & store. Let the egg bites cool in the pan for 5 minutes before removing. Enjoy immediately, or store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 5 days.

meal prep & reheating

  • To reheat from the fridge: microwave 2 egg bites on a paper towel for 30–40 seconds until warmed through.
  • Pro tip: pair 2 egg bites with a slice of toasted sprouted grain bread for a fast, balanced breakfast with ~25g+ of protein.
